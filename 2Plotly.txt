Countries in Western Africa have extremely varying populations, but their population changes appear to have similar trends. This graphic hows the population of each African country in 1965, 1985, and 2005. Most of the countries in the selected area (and throughout Africa, if you move the map) saw a smaller increase in population between 1965 and 1985 than between 1985 and 2005, indicating that both small and large African countries are growing.
